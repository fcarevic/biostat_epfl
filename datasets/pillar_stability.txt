Dataset:  pillar_stability.dat

Source: R.K. Wattimena, S. Kramadibrata, I.D. Sidi, M.A. Azizi (2013).

International Journal of Rock Mechanics & Mining Sciences," Vol. 58,
pp. 55-60.
